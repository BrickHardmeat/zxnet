96E6: PAUSER
Used by the routines at subversion_spell, main_routine, TREPUT and display_wizard_spell_range.
Loop 40800 times to delay for approximately 0.15 seconds.
PAUSER 96E6 PUSH BC Preserve BC.
96E7 LD B,$FF Set B to 255 as outer loop counter.
PAUSER_0 96E9 PUSH BC Preserve outer loop counter.
96EA LD B,$A0 Set B to 160 as inner loop counter.
PAUSER_1 96EC DJNZ PAUSER_1 Loop back to PAUSER_1 for 160 iterations.
96EE POP BC Restore outer loop counter.
96EF DJNZ PAUSER_0 Loop back to PAUSER_0 for 255 iterations.
96F1 POP BC Restore BC and return.
96F2 RET
